Facts I bet you did not know:

1. So you might think Owls can turn their heads 360 degrees, right? Well, this is not exactly true. Yes, they can turn their heads quite a lot but, most Owls can turn their heads 270 degrees max. A specific owl called the Barred Owl can turn it's head just over 270 degrees.

2. Did you know that there are over half the amount of species of owls than there are of dogs worldwide? Owls being at 236. Dogs being at 406!

#1 Snowy Owls (Polar Owls)

Fun Facts:

  1. The Snowy Owl's scientific name is Bubo Scandica.
  2. Snowy Owls are carnivores (only eats meat).
  3. Snowy Owl are solitary (if in mating season they are more social).
  4. The avarage lifespan of this owl is 10 years.
  5. The Snowy Owl is 19-28 inches and their wingspan is 4.2-4.8 feet.
  6. The weight of a snowy owl is 3.5-6.8 pounds.
  7. The colours are white and black.
  8. The Snowy Owl lives in the arctic of North America and Palearctic(all live in Tundra).
